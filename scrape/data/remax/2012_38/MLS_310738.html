Description
This custom-designed ocean view home is nestled on almost one acre of property and is located in the Mt. Newton valley on a private road. This unique home was created by Architect Ben Levinson to be contemporary yet timeless. Southern exposure and views of Brentwood Bay will not distract from the well-appointed interior. A formal living/dining room, large kitchen with two sinks, sunny breakfast nook adjacent to a large family room, media room and office complete the main floor. A winding staircase leads to the master suite, two bedrooms and exercise room on the upper floor. A nanny suite on the lower level adds further to this amazing home. Many special extras make this home a must-see.
