"1211 BEACH DRIVE!". This was the address for families retiring from "THE UPLANDS". They wanted the best waterfront in OAK BAY and they wanted to walk home from the "VICTORIA GOLF CLUB", after 18 holes and a good meal. There is not a unit in comparison for such a tranquil setting and a feeling of privacy.
