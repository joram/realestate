Description
Providing a truly authentic country living experience, this unique and lovingly cared for "larger-parcel" Ardmore property is a rare find! Drive through the treed entrance, and step back to a quieter, gentler time. Within walking distance to beautiful beaches, the c.1911 home is in charmingly original condition, yet has undergone major upgrades to all the mechanical systems, and boasts a full-height above ground basement, just awaiting your finishing touches. The level, well drained acreage features a 4 stall barn, large riding ring and paddock area, plus a delightful stand-alone cottage. The owners have also generated modest income from a market garden stall, featuring all natural "on-site-grown" produce and fruit. A VERY special property!
