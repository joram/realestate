Description
Historic Otter Point Farm is a story-book like setting of 35.62 verdant acres of working Llama & Alpaca farm bordered by pristine King Creek & just 8 min from Sooke Centre. In the past, this property has supported large scale sheep, dairy, beef, hay production, market gardens & equestrian centre. Lush organic pasture lands are fenced & x-fenced. Huge barns w/lofts, shelters & out bldgs. Owners' residences includes a custom 2002, 3BR, 2BA, 2086sf w/hardwood floors, vaulted ceiling & unfin bsmt; a lovely 4BR, 2BA, 3085sf home w/separate studio & a delightful 1BR, 575sf guest cottage w/hot tub. A must see for the discerning farmer/rancher, extended family or group passionate about country living, self sustainability, organic farming & revenue.
