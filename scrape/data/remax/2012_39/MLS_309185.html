Description
Prepare to be impressed when you view this bright and spacious Broadmead executive home. This residence has benefited greatly from a full architectural remodel by Jan Zak in 1993. This tranquil home has a distinct Westcoast open concept feel with multiple levels, wood floors and natural light from large windows and skylights. Well thought out floorplan includes formal living room, dining room, modern kitchen with island and eating area, family room, 4 bedrooms including a private master with huge ensuite, walk in closet and access to hot tub. The lower level features a large bedroom with ensuite and kitchenette perfect for nanny or guests. Private yard with plenty of decks to follow the sun. Newer roof & 2 car garage. A pleasure to show.
