Description
Sunny,South facing low bank waterfront, spectacular sunrises & lots of privacy, tucked away on a quiet lane. The home was designed & renovated to maximize the water & garden views & allows for private and light filled oceanfront living with large decks, lovely patio & easy access to the walk-on beach. A 'hidden' office under the eaves completes this serene space.
