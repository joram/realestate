First Time on the Market Ever! A sheltered Waterfront property with M5 zoning & a rare, legal, foreshore dock in North Saanich, for private boat moorage & marine recreation off lovely Curteis Point. The large dock has been completely rebuilt, offers deep water moorage and can house a number of boats. Active marine life in a peaceful & outstanding location.
