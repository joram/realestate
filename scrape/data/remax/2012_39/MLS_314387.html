Description
Large charming Spanish Mediterranean inspired designed home, has an expansive 3 levels floorplan and is located in a premium coveted Arbutus neighborhood on a little niche of fine homes. Parklike gardens with beautiful patios, trees + shrubs. Wonderful sun exposure and water glimpses. The main level features a grand foyer with high ceilings, curved staircase and dramatic space, oversized formal principal rooms for entertaining / family, family room + sunroom are off the kitchen area. The lower floor features a deluxe 2 bdrm inlaw suite with its own laundry + storage. Walk to Arbutus Cove + UVIC.
