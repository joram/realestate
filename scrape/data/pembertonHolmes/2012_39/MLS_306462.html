Occupying the West Side of Turgoose Point, this Boaters Dream Property offers your own private dock & mooring buoys to take advantage of the ideal location just minutes to Sidney Spit, Salt Spring & Pender Island & many other Gulf Island adventures, while only 8 minutes from Victoria Airport. This very rare property offers approx 213? of low bank waterfront, meticulously landscaped gardens with numerous patios and decks to observe marine wildlife and enjoy the all day sun with its western exposure. The residence was designed and built by one of Western Canada's most influential architects, John A De Castri & labeled his ?Century House? with 12 ? high ceilings, expansive windows & spectacular views of Sidney, the Gulf Islands & beyond
