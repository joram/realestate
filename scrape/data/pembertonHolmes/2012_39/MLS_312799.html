Extensively upgraded, stand alone, historic downtown office building with parking for 10 vehicles. Designed by Rattenbury & originally built for the BC Electric Railway in 1905, this property can accommodate a single or multiple office tenants with potential retail space on the main floor which has a loading dock. Located on highly sought after Fort St, 1 block from Harbour at intersection of Fort & Langley Streets. Excellent pedestrian traffic & visibility with good quality interior tenant improvements. Ideal for a high tech user, this property has electronic access controls, video surveillance, secure Cat 6 cable connection, emergency power generator, Level 3 data vault + 3 server rooms and it is serviced by two fibre optics providers.
